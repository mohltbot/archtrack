# LinkedIn Post - Accounting/Tax Skill Launch

---

**I built something I'm actually excited about.**

As a CPA working in AI advisory, I've been frustrated by most "accounting automation" tools. They're either:
- Built by developers who never took an accounting class
- Way too complex for what most firms actually need
- Don't handle real-world edge cases (looking at you, partial period reconciliations)

So I built my own.

It's an OpenClaw skill for accounting and tax professionals that actually understands accounting:

✅ **Bank reconciliation** - Auto-matches GL to bank statements, finds missing transactions, flags duplicates

✅ **Tax calculations** - Federal tax, self-employment, quarterly estimates. 2024 brackets built in

✅ **Financial analysis** - Ratios, variance analysis, budget vs actual with smart alerts

✅ **Depreciation** - Straight-line, MACRS, all the methods you actually use

The cool part? It's all decimal-precise (no floating point errors), runs locally on my Mac mini, and learns from your data.

Why am I posting this?

Because the accounting profession is changing fast. Firms that embrace AI automation will outcompete those still doing everything manually. But the tools have to actually work for accountants - not just impress developers.

This is my contribution to moving the profession forward. Free to use, open source, and I'll keep updating it as tax laws change.

If you're a CPA exploring AI, would love your feedback. And if you're building in this space - let's connect. There's a lot of whitespace in accounting AI that nobody's tackling yet.
