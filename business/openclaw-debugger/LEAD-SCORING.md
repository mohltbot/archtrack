# Lead Scoring Framework

*A systematic approach to prioritizing OpenClaw Debugger leads*

---

## Scoring Criteria

Leads are scored on a 100-point scale across 4 dimensions:

### 1. Pain Level (0-30 points)
How urgent is their problem?

| Score | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| 30 | Production down, revenue impacted, "urgent help needed" |
| 20 | Workflow blocked, multiple days stuck, tried many solutions |
| 10 | Annoyance/friction, workaround exists |
| 5 | Nice-to-have, curiosity-driven |

### 2. Technical Fit (0-25 points)
How well do they match our expertise?

| Score | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| 25 | OpenClaw + specific integration (Discord, Telegram, Sonos, etc.) |
| 20 | OpenClaw core functionality issue |
| 15 | Related tool (n8n, Home Assistant, self-hosted infra) |
| 10 | Generic AI/automation question |
| 5 | Off-topic or unclear scope |

### 3. Engagement Quality (0-25 points)
How invested are they in solving this?

| Score | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| 25 | Detailed error logs, specific configs, clear reproduction steps |
| 20 | Multiple messages, follow-up questions, active thread |
| 15 | Responded to initial outreach |
| 10 | Single message with some detail |
| 5 | Vague "help me" with no context |

### 4. Conversion Potential (0-20 points)
Likelihood to become a paying client?

| Score | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| 20 | Business use case mentioned, "willing to pay", time-sensitive |
| 15 | Professional context (startup, agency, consultant) |
| 10 | Power user vibes (technical depth, complex setup) |
| 5 | Hobbyist/personal project |
| 0 | Student, "just exploring" |

---

## Score Interpretation

| Total Score | Priority | Action |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 80-100 | 🔥🔥🔥 HOT | Immediate response, offer consultation, fast-track |
| 60-79 | 🔥🔥 WARM | Respond within 24h, provide value, nurture to hot |
| 40-59 | 🔥 LUKEWARM | Standard response, add to nurture sequence |
| <40 | ❄️ COLD | Low priority, batch responses, educational content only |

## Action Framework

### For HOT Leads (80+)
- [ ] Respond within 2 hours during business hours
- [ ] Offer free 15-min diagnostic call
- [ ] Provide specific, actionable fix
- [ ] Follow up within 48 hours

### For WARM Leads (60-79)
- [ ] Respond within 24 hours
- [ ] Share relevant resources + case study
- [ ] Invite to office hours/community
- [ ] Re-score after next interaction

### For LUKEWARM Leads (40-59)
- [ ] Batch response weekly
- [ ] Point to documentation/tutorials
- [ ] Add to newsletter if available
- [ ] Check back in 2 weeks

### For COLD Leads (<40)
- [ ] Monthly check-in
- [ ] Educational content only
- [ ] Deprioritize unless they re-engage

---

## Weekly Lead Review Checklist

- [ ] Score any new leads from the past week
- [ ] Re-score leads with new interactions
- [ ] Identify HOT leads needing immediate attention
- [ ] Move cold leads to nurture sequence
- [ ] Update conversion tracking (leads → calls → clients)
